Frequently Asked Questions

The answers to everything you need to know before spending a wonderful day with the bride and groom!

Can I bring my children?

We love each and every one of your children; however, we are having an ADULTS-ONLY event. We appreciate your understanding and support of our wishes on this matter.

Can I bring a plus one?

We cannot accommodate guests not otherwise specified on your invitation. Please note that a "number of seats reserved" will be provided on your RSVP card. We can't wait to celebrate with you!

When should I rsvp by?

Please RSVP by August 30th, 2024.

Where is the ceremony and when should I arrive?

The ceremony is being held at Sorella Farms, 171 Little Ln. Evington, Virginia 24550. It will start promptly at 4:30pm. If you are driving yourself, please arrive 15 minutes early as we will not let anyone attend the ceremony if they are late. The ceremony will be held outside and the reception will be inside of a barn.

Is there transportation provided?

Yes, we will have transportation picking guests up at the Virginian Hotel in downtown Lynchburg promptly at 3:00pm. We will also have transportation taking guests back to the Virginian Hotel after the Send Off at 9:30pm. There will also be parking on-site for those who wish to drive themselves.

What color is the bridal party wearing?

The bridal party will be wearing dusty sage green.

What should I wear?

Formal attire, please. We want our family and friends to dress-up for the occasion and feel great! Please avoid wearing sage green and white.

What will be served?

We will have a cocktail hour that will start immediately after the ceremony with hors d'oeuvres. There will be an open bar with beer, wine, seltzer, and two signature drinks. We will have a sit-down buffet-style dinner.

Is this an unplugged ceremony?

Please note that our ceremony will be an unplugged ceremony. We kindly ask that phones and cameras remain out of site until the ceremony conlusion. We have a team of photographers and videographers capturing every moment.
