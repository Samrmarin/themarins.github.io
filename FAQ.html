Frequently Asked Questions

The answers to everything you need to know before spending a wonderful day with the bride and groom!

Can I bring my children?

We love each and everyone of your children; however, we are having an ADULTS-ONLY event. We appreciate your understanding and support of our wishes on this matter.

Can I bring a plus one?

We cannot accommodate guests not otherwise specified on your invitation. Please note that a "number of seats reserved" will be provided on your RSVP card. We can't wait to celebrate with you!

When should I rsvp by?

Yes, here will go another question.

Where is the ceremony and when should I arrive?

The ceremony is being held at Sorella Farms in Evington, Virginia. It will state promptly at ___pm.

Is there transportation provided?

Yes, we will have transportation picking guests up at the Virginian Hotel in downtown Lynchburg promptly at __pm. We will also have transportation taking guests back to the Virginian Hotel after the Send Off at 9:00pm. There will also be parking on-site for those who wish to drive themselves.

What color is the bridal party wearing?

The bridal party will be wearing dusty sage green.

What should I wear?

Formal attire, please. We want our family and friends to dress-up for the occasion and feel great!

What will be served?

We will have a cocktail hour that will start immediately after the ceremony with hors d'oeuvres. Followed by a sit-down dinner, as well as an open bar with beer, wine, and two signature drinks.
